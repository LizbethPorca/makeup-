Makeup

Makeup is made primarily of water, preservatives, thickening agents, emulsifiers, moisturizers, coloring agents, and fragrances. Depending on the chemistry behind the product, it can have a range of health impacts ranging from hormone disruption to potential carcinogenic effects.Makeup is something that people use to make their face look different and to use to make different styles on your face .
